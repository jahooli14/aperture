🧹 Clear Pupils Cache

If you're experiencing a white screen or the app won't load, this tool will clear all cached data and service workers.

⚠️ Note: This will log you out and you'll need to sign in again.
What this does:
  • Unregisters all service workers
  • Clears all browser caches
  • Clears localStorage (except auth)
  • Forces a fresh reload
